The Hornet looks like an oval with black and yellow stripes and a dark yellow outline. It has two antennae at the front, which appear as slightly curved black lines. It also has a Missile at the back.

Speed: Slow when passive, Normal when aggressive.

Behavior: When firing at the player, hornets turn 180 degrees and fire the "missile" attached to their body.

The stats here are for the common hornet, scale as needed.

health: 62.5
damage: 50
Armor: 1
Missile hp: 5
Missile damage: 10
