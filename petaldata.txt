## RICE

Rice is a legacy petal in florr.io, added on November 2, 2020. It has weak damage and health stats, but reloads almost instantly. Rice is dropped by Baby Ants.

Rice appears as a white petal in the shape of an elongated, thinner jellybean with a light-gray border, slightly larger than Light.

## GLASS

Glass is a petal in florr.io. It phases through entities and cannot damage the same entity more often than once per second. Glass is dropped by Sandstorms and Soldier Ants. The faster the player moves, the more damage the glass should do. Nerf the starting stats of the glass from the .csv file, so moving faster is balanced.

As its name implies, Glass is a shard of a glass. While in-game, it can morph into many different shapes: it can be assumed that the game draws 5 different points, then joins them and fills in the outline with a transparent white color. Its petal icon displays one of its various forms: an irregular pentagonal shape

## LEAF

Leaf is a legacy petal in florr.io, added on May 14, 2020. It heals the user passively by a set amount of HP per second. Leaf is dropped by Baby Ants, Centipedes, Leafbugs and Worker Ants.

Leaf's appearance is true to its name: it's a leaf shaped petal, somewhat resembling an oval, but is pointed on one end. It is green with a darker green outline. In addition, a dark green stripe is located in the middle that runs down most of the length of the leaf. A dark green rectangle (the stem) is located at the rounded end of the oval.

## CORN

Corn is a legacy petal in florr.io, added on November 2, 2020. It has a long respawn time and a relatively low damage stat, but an extremely high health stat. Corn is dropped by Worker Ants and Worker Fire Ants.

Corn looks like a yellow tooth-shaped form, and its borders are even more yellow, resembling a piece of corn.
